Steak

A T-bone steak.

This T-bone steak marinade was one of my grandfather's favorite recipes. It's so simple, the family loves it, and it's a wonderful recipe for a great barbecue meal!

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup vegetable oil.
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce.
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice.
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic, and pepper together in a bowl until combined.
  2. Place meat in a resealable plastic bag, coat with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Discard marinade after it has been used.
